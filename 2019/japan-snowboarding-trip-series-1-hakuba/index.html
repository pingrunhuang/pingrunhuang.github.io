During the end of the year 2019, I have decided to travel to Japan which is a brand new contry where I have not reached any before. Here is the whole picture of this trip.

So most of the destinations of this trip is towards Kansai area which include Osaka, Kyoto and also Nara

Most of the time, when you decided to travel around Japan. The very first choice to buy a JR Pass which has been widely talked about in the internet. But since my destination is to snowboard in Hakuba, so I decided to buy a bus ticket which allow me to travel from Osaka to Hakuba Happo bus terminal from end to end without other extra transition.
