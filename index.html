HDFS Summary

I recently came across a lot of interviews when I applied to the big data developer position. Lots of interviewers asked me about the architecture of HDFS and I was a bit confused. So I decided to write a blog to sort the things out.

HDFS is a distributed file system implementing the paper of google file system. The component of HDFS includes NameNode, DataNode and Secondary NameNode. HDFS is also implementing the architecture of Master and Slave mode. The NameNode is basically the master and DataNode is the slave. The Secondary NameNode is kind of the back up of NameNode just in case the NameNode get crashed. The DataNode is will send the heartbeat to NameNode saying if it is alive or dead.
