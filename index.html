Bayes statistics summary

Bayes’ equation

In a nutshell, Bayes’ equation is a result of conditional probability:
P[ A,B ] = P[ A|B ] P[ B ] = P[ B|A ] P[ A ]
Notice: P[ A,B ] means P[ A&&B ] which is also means the probability of event A and B happens.
