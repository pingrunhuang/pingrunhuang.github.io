Nocturnal Animals

Nocturnal Animals is a movie telling 3 stories in parallel. Amy Adams and Jake Gyllenhaal are the two main characters which are called Susan and Edward respectively in the movie. It is the most emotional movie that I have seen in 2016. One of the story inside the movie is a script which is like a reflection to the marriage of Susan. Although it is kind of violent, it is still worth watching.
