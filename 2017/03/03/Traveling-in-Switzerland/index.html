Traveling in Switzerland

I spent one week traveling in Switzerland at February 2017. Since the temperature is pretty low, I also traveled with my snowboard. In this journal, I will not only talk about the tourisms of Switzerland but also the snowboarding area that I visited during my trip.

I took a plane from Paris to Zurich on February 4th 2017. After arriving at Zurich air port. I took a train called IC line from Zurich to Bern where my friend lives there. The price of the train is so high in Switzerland. My friend recommended me with a travel ticket called Interrail Pass which can be used to travel with all the public transportation but the price is relatively low comparing to the SBB ticket.
The weather of the very first day in Switzerland was not that good with heavily snowing and cloudy sky. I spent the whole day in Bern.

I highly recommend this stop for skiing spot for those who are very skilled in skiing because it is quite steep and full of concave.
