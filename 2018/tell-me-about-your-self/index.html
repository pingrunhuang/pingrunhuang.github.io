Tell me about your self
ad
Jan 15, 2018
2 minutes read

Hi, I’m running. I grew up in shenzhen and now also working here. About me in a nutshell, I would say I’m just a normal person with curiosity. I’ve always trying to figure out the basic principle behind technologies and also share what I know with others. That is why I keep writing blogs and choose Computer science as my major and career.

about one and a half years ago, I started my internship in paris. Working as a software developer in a company called scaled risk who provide software built on top of HBASE for storing and analyzing huge amount of data, my job there is to get familiar with how the product connect to hadoop eco system and find a way to deploy our product on the cluster more efficiently. That’s how I got involved in the big data area.

Later on, I came back to China and started my first full-time job in shenzhen. The company that I’m working for is called touchstone who provide services for querying credential data. Most of my job there is to make sure the data got transferred from production environment to the offline analytic environment day by day.

But now, I’m confronted limits since the company is owned by the nation and the hierarchy is quite obvious which makes it very time consuming to do something. The company size is not that large though which makes it impossible to reach out for different kinds of people. So generally speaking, the working environment is not what I am looking for. So I decided to find a new job in the industry. Shopee is good for me at the first glance. International, open-minded and fast-growing, I figure out these are the core features that I’m looking forward to work with. And my overseas learning and working experience in the big data area might be a good fit in to this position.

Except the working part of me, I also love to go to gym after work. I hope I can find some more interesting people sharing the same hobby with me in Shopee. I do like communicating with others and learn what they do. Therefore, I go to the tech conference or startup salon very often to meet people who use technologies to change their lives and make the world a better place. Finally, I do hope even if I did not get the job, we can still remain contact to communicate about advanced technologies and how we use it to impact our lives.
