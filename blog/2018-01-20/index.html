Some thoughts about applying fancy tech into the insurance industry -- review from Bee+ event
ad
Jan 20, 2018
4 minutes read

Human are always excited about advanced tech that make the world a better place

I have been honored to join the event hosted by bee+ and BNP on tonight. It was quite surprise that the event went so well that brought me into lots of thoughts which is what I want to share.

First thing first, there were basically 3 topics that brought me into deep thinking: - How does your technical background get applied to the fields? - What is the real meaning of blockchain in the real market? - Think about the long term goal for your startup.

How does your technical background get applied to the fields?

This thought was actually came up into my mind when I heard a lot of application of advanced technologies in the insurance fields. I really like the explanation came up with Derek who is a maker based in shenzhen probably because we have the same background in engineer. He said that having a background in insurance is better but not necessary. All you can do is find someone who has the in fields knowledge to work with you. Apart from that, Yasunori from BNP Paribas also mentioned that technolies should just be a tool, what should be achieved is make a better product or help increase the user experience and came up with some examples. I totally agree with these brilliant ideas. So I think insurance is kind of saling people to buy some thing to make your life get some guarantees which may cause some rejections for some reason. But nowadays, with new technologies come into play, is there some way we can let the user really experience the product or pushing the right product to the right person? I think this is all the products from all industries should consider about.

What is the real meaning of blockchain in the real market?

Think of blockchain as a eco-system where users in it could use the coin defined by the eco-system. This was actually an answer from a question that I asked from the CEO of family points called Ainar who use blockchain under the hood for saling there parenting product. At the very beginning of blockchain, I think the main purpose of it is to make the transaction transparent with the so called open ledger. But as this technology become so populer, all the industry suddenlly want to build products on top of blockchain which I think might just be a way to winning the investment. The same questions goes to familypoints. I was always wondering is it really necessary to use the blockchain? Why not just use the traditional way. But Ainar really inspire me. In the real world, blockchain is not just a transparent database. What we can do about it is build your own eco-system so that users can use your customized coin or token to enjoy your service. In this way, user will really get stick to your product once you build up their buying custom. All you need to do is let them join your chain.

Think about the long term goal for your startup.

This actually a talk between me and Derek who is the CEO of LUMENCHACHE. He mentioned that lots of investors might just want to make money despite the quality of the products they invested. As a CEO with technical background, you not only have to find the investor but you also have to pick the right one. You have to think about the future of your product. In which time do you think is the right time to get a satisfying product. If you and the investors don’t agree with that, you should just let go of that funding because that won’t be a good thing for your company. Maybe it’s because the engineering background that we both share, his suggestion really inspire me for further thought about how to combine technique, product and bussiness and how important it is to manage them well.

Finally, this is a good event and thanks BEE for hosting this great event for us, cheers!
